Ingredients
Salad
- Watermelon, cubed
- Red onions, thinly sliced
- Mint, chopped
- Kalamata olives, pitted and rougly chopped
- Feta cheese, crumbled

Dressing
- Juice of 1 lemon
- Olive oil
- Salt and pepper, to taste

Combine all salad ingredients in a bowl.
Drizzle with olive oil, then add lemon juice.
Toss salad in dressing, then add salt and pepper to taste
